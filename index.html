Utah

Utah is a beautiful state full of nature and wildlife. You can see everything from desert, to snow capped mountains, and everything in between. Utah is known for its 5 national parks including Zion, Arches, Canyonlands, Bryce Canyon, Capitol Reef. Utah was admitted into the Union on January 4th 1896 making it the 45th state. Preceding this, the Pioneers reached and settled utah in 1847.
