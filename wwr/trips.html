Highlighted Trips

Tara River Half Day Adventure

The Tara River Half Day Adventure is our beginner package. This is in a calm section of river which will is beginner friendly. This trip begins at 8:00 AM and you will be finished by 1:30 PM. Lunch will be provided.

Tully River 1 Day Adventure

The Tully River 1 Day Adventure is intended for intermediate adventurers. The section of the river we raft ranges from class 1 - 3 rapids so you will be required to maneuver around objects and work as a team. This trip begins at 8:00 AM and you will not be back until 9:00 PM. Lunch and dinner will be provided.

Salmon River 7 Day Adventure

The Salmon River 7 Day Adventure is intended for hardcore adventurers only. This trek traverses through class 4 rapids. This is not for the faint of heart. This trip will begin Monday at 7:30 AM and will finish the next monday at 12:00 PM. Complimentary paddle board yoga lessons will be provided. All meals will be provided.
